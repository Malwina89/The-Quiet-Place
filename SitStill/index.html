Sit still for a moment

As work is important for your survival, so is rest for a peaceful mind.


If you are burnt out and struggling, you don't need more productivity. What you really need is a break. Humans are designed to work efficiently but not continuously. After a certain time, our body does not support us and we fail to reflect efficiency in our work.

Why do you need a break?
A rest break is something which boosts up your mind and soul, energizing them in a positive way. Rest breaks at work/ home are usually called as “tea breaks” or “crib breaks”. They are absolutely important, especially during work hour.

How to take an effective break?

  • Firstable, don't feel guilty about your break.
  • Go outside, if you can. Even few minutes walk has a great impact on your mind.
  • Get some physical activity - there is always time for few squats and stretching.
  • Exercise your eyes: if you look at a screen most of the day, your eyes could use a break as well. Every 20 minutes look at object that is 20 feet away from you for at least 20 seconds.
  • Give your brain a break. Simply do nothing for 3 minutes.
