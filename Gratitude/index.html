Be Grateful

How often do you pause to appreciate what you have in life?


It can be easy to get swept away in the fast lane and forget to stop and show your appreciation for what you do have. A life well lived is one of gratitude and thankfulness.

Gratitude doesn’t have to be saved for the “big” things in life. The habit of being grateful starts with appreciating every good thing in life and recognizing that there is nothing too small for you to be thankful for. - Janet Miller for 'Forbes'

Why should you be grateful?
You should be grateful because knowing that you have gifts or things in your life to be thankful for will help you to become successful. You become aware of things that you have in your life other people might not have. Such awareness itself is going to help strengthen your character.

What happens when you are not grateful?
When you are not grateful, you begin to experience envy, jealousy, and frustration that only bring you down. At this point, you start forgetting how far you have come. When you let your desires control you, your life is at risk of having a low purpose.
